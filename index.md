## 🛠️ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### The game is lagging or crashing. What should I do?
Please ensure your device is updated to the latest iOS version and try restarting the app.

### How do I reset my leaderboard score?
Leaderboard scores are linked to your Apple Game Center account. You can manage your profile directly in your iOS Settings app under Game Center.
