# Privacy Policy for Roof Master

**Effective Date: June 26, 2026**

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles user data, particularly concerning our online leaderboard and advertising partners.

## 1. Information We Collect & How We Use It

### A. Online Leaderboard Data
To operate our global competition system and display player rankings, we collect and store limited gameplay data:
*   **Username / Nickname:** The custom name you choose to display publicly on the leaderboard.
*   **Game Scores:** Your highest score, roofing completion times, and gameplay metrics.
*   **Identifiers:** A unique, randomly generated user ID assigned to your device to link your scores to your profile. This is not tied to your real-world identity.

### B. Device & Usage Information
We automatically collect technical data necessary to maintain app stability, fix bugs, and optimize performance:
*   **Crash Logs & Diagnostics:** Information about app crashes or performance anomalies to improve gameplay.

## 2. Third-Party Advertising
Our app displays third-party advertisements to keep the game free to play. These advertising partners may collect and process device data to serve and measure ads:
*   **Data Collected:** Ad networks may collect device identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA), IP addresses, location data, and ad interaction metrics.
*   **Personalization:** If you grant permission via the iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, these networks may use this data to deliver personalized ads tailored to your interests. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will be generic and less relevant.

## 3. Data Retention and Security
Your leaderboard nickname and scores are stored securely on our servers for as long as you keep the app installed. We implement industry-standard measures to protect this limited data from unauthorized access.

## 4. Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted leaderboard data or generated a profile without parental consent, please contact us so we can delete it.
